7 Cheese Mac n' Cheese


Creamy oven baked mac n' cheese

Sappy Background Story

As a child, I loved visiting my grandmother and eating her creamy 7 cheese mac and cheese. She would spend hours shredding each type of cheese and whisking together the milk and cream until they were perfectly combined. While she worked, she would share stories of her childhood on a farm, and I would often help her in the kitchen. Now, whenever I make her recipe, I feel a sense of warmth and nostalgia, remembering the stories she shared and the memories we made together. It's a dish that will always hold a special place in my heart.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Gouda cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Fontina cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Asiago cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Colby Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the elbow macaroni according to package instructions until it is al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk constantly for 1-2 minutes, until the mixture is smooth and bubbly.
  4. Gradually whisk in the whole milk and heavy cream. Add the garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, cayenne pepper, salt, and black pepper. Continue whisking until the mixture thickens, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Add the cheddar cheese, Gouda cheese, Fontina cheese, Parmesan cheese, Asiago cheese, Mozzarella cheese, and Colby Jack cheese. Stir until the cheeses are melted and the sauce is smooth.
  6. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce and stir to coat.
  7. Transfer the mac and cheese to a large baking dish. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until the top is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let the mac and cheese cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your creamy 7 cheese mac and cheese!
